# Code of Conduct

SDK Parity follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in SDK Parity projects a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear and specific.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Accept maintainer decisions when a thread has reached a conclusion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or community spaces.
- Any behavior that would reasonably make participation unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the project.

Report conduct concerns through the security or support channels listed in this organization.
